Adobo

Description

Pork Adobo is a famous dish in the Philippines.

Because of its great taste, it is loved by the Filipinos, and even foreigners.

Ingredients:

  • Pork Meat
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Water
  • Bay Leaves
  • Pepper
  • Pork Cubes
  • Garlic
  • Onion

    • Procedures:

    1. Wash the pork meat then marinate it in soy sauce and pepper for 1 hour.
    2. Saute the garlic and onion.
    3. Add the marinated pork meat.
    4. Add 1 cups of water. Cook until boil.
    5. Add a little bit of vinegar.
    6. Add pork cubes and 2 Bay Leaves.
    7. Let it simmer and serve it hot.
