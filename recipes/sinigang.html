Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a classic Filipino soup characterized by its sour and savory medley of flavors.

It's popular comfort food in the Philippines, usually served on its own or paired with steamed rice on rainy days to ward off the cold.

Ingredients:

  • Pork Meat
  • Tamarind
  • Fish Sauce
  • Water
  • Gabi
  • Radish
  • Vegetables
  • Finger Chili Peppers

    • Procedures:

    1. Rinse pork ribs and drain well.
    2. In a pot over medium heat, combine pork and enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, skimming scum that accumulates on top.
    3. Using a fork, mash the tamarinds to release the pulp.
    4. Once broth clears, add tomatoes, onion, and fish sauce. Lower heat and simmer for about 1 to 1 Â½ hours or until meat is tender, adding more water as necessary to maintain about 8 cups.
    5. Add gabi and cook for about 4 to 6 minutes or until tender.
    6. Add chili peppers and radish. Continue to simmer for about 2 to 3 minutes.
    7. Add long beans. Continue to cook for about 2 minutes.
    8. Add bok choy and continue to cook for about 1 minute. Serve hot.
